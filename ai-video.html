FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can you produce AI video content?
Most batches are delivered within 2 hours of final brief approval. Complex projects with custom avatars or advanced voice cloning may require 4–6 hours for initial rendering, but subsequent variants from the same brief render in minutes.
What quality can AI-generated video achieve?
Broadcast-quality output at up to 4K resolution. Our diffusion models are fine-tuned on platform-native content, so AI reels are optimized for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube compression algorithms — often outperforming manually shot content in clarity and engagement.
How does avatar localization work?
We clone your brand ambassador or create a custom avatar, then use AI lip-sync and voice synthesis to localize the same video into 42+ languages. The avatar's lip movements, facial expressions, and gestures are re-rendered for each language — no re-shooting required.
How many variants can you produce per month?
There is no practical cap. Our rendering pipeline supports unlimited parallelization. We routinely produce 1,000+ variants per month for enterprise clients, each with different hooks, backgrounds, CTAs, aspect ratios, and languages.
Will the content look generic or robotic?
Not at all. Our models are trained on UGC-style content and real creator footage, so output feels authentic and native to each platform. We also fine-tune outputs on your brand assets, ensuring every variant matches your visual identity and tone.
