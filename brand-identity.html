FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a brand identity project include?
A complete brand identity project includes logo design (primary, secondary, and submark variations), color palette with design tokens, typography scale and hierarchy, brand guidelines documentation, social media templates, stationery design, and a comprehensive asset library organized for easy access and application.
How long does a brand identity project take?
Typical brand identity projects take 4-6 weeks from discovery to final delivery. This includes research and strategy (1-2 weeks), design exploration and refinement (2-3 weeks), and final guidelines documentation and asset delivery (1 week). Rush timelines are available for an additional fee.
Can you redesign our existing brand?
Absolutely. We handle both new brand creation and rebrand projects. For rebrands, we conduct a brand audit to identify what's working and what needs evolution, then develop a strategic approach that honors your existing equity while modernizing for your target market.
What file formats do you deliver?
We deliver logos in SVG, PNG, and PDF formats at multiple sizes. Brand guidelines are provided as interactive PDF and Figma files. All design tokens are delivered in CSS, JSON, and Figma variable formats for seamless integration with your development workflow.
Do you offer ongoing brand support?
Yes, we offer retainer packages for ongoing brand management, including new asset creation, brand guideline updates, seasonal campaign design, and brand consultation. This ensures your brand stays consistent and evolves strategically as your business grows.
