Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Introduction

At Digital Advertise, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and share information when you visit our website, submit audit requests, subscribe to our newsletter, or use our digital advertising and AI marketing services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, and physical company address.
  • Business Details: Company name, website URL, existing marketing channels, and monthly ad budgets.
  • Communications: Any feedback, messages, or meeting details provided through our Calendly widget or contact form.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device specs, browser details, and navigation metrics collected automatically via cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We utilize your data to deliver services, specifically to:

  • Provide site audits, campaign metrics reviews, and channel consultation.
  • Respond to customer service and contact inquiries.
  • Send newsletters, product announcements, and advertising updates (which you can opt-out of at any time).
  • Optimize our web layout, graphics, and performance metrics via analytics tools.

4. Data Protection and Security

We deploy strict technical and organizational safeguards (including SSL encryption, secure API channels, and restricted database permissions) to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or exposure of your business credentials.

5. Your Data Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, deletion of, or changes to the personal records we maintain. You can contact us directly at privacy@digitaladvertise.com to exercise these rights.
