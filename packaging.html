FAQ

Common questions

What file formats do you deliver for print?

We deliver press-ready PDFs with CMYK/Pantone profiles, bleed marks, and dieline overlays. You'll also receive editable source files in Adobe Illustrator and InDesign formats.

Do you handle structural prototyping?

Yes. We engineer dielines and provide structural specifications for die-cut manufacturers. For physical prototypes, we coordinate with partner facilities for die-cut samples.

Can you design for Amazon and D2C at the same time?

Absolutely. We design packaging systems that work across Amazon listing imagery, D2C unboxing, and retail shelf presence — ensuring brand consistency at every touchpoint.

How long does a packaging project take?

A typical single-SKU project takes 2–3 weeks from strategy to print-ready delivery. Full product line redesigns (5–15 SKUs) typically take 4–6 weeks depending on scope.

Do you offer sustainable packaging options?

Yes. We specialize in eco-friendly material sourcing including FSC-certified papers, soy-based inks, and recyclable structural designs that meet sustainability goals without compromising shelf impact.
