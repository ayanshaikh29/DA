FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How quickly will I see results from performance marketing?
Most clients see measurable improvements within 30–60 days. Initial gains come from tracking fixes (CAPI recovery) and campaign restructuring. ROAS typically compounds as creative testing data accumulates over 90+ days.
What platforms do you manage?
We manage Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Performance Max), Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and programmatic display networks. Each platform gets a tailored strategy based on your audience and goals.
How does server-side tracking (CAPI) help?
iOS privacy changes and ad blockers cause 15–25% of conversion data to disappear from browser-based tracking. Our server-side Conversions API sends conversion events directly from your server to ad platforms, recovering that lost data and improving campaign optimization accuracy.
What's the minimum ad spend to get started?
We recommend a minimum of $5,000/month in ad spend for Google and Meta campaigns. This allows enough budget for meaningful creative testing and audience optimization. For TikTok and LinkedIn, we recommend $3,000/month minimum.
How do you report on performance?
You get a live attribution dashboard plus weekly performance reports and monthly executive summaries. Reports cover ROAS, CPA, conversion volume, creative performance, and budget pacing — all tied to your actual business metrics, not vanity numbers.
