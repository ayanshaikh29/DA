FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What types of video content do you produce?
We produce commercial brand films, social media video content (Reels, TikToks, Shorts), animated explainer videos, product demo videos, corporate films, testimonial videos, and motion graphics — all optimized for their target platform and audience.
How long does a typical video production take?
A standard social media video package (5-10 Reels) takes 7-10 business days from scripting to delivery. Full brand films and commercials typically require 3-4 weeks, including pre-production, filming, post-production, and revision rounds.
Do you handle everything from scripting to final delivery?
Yes. Our end-to-end pipeline covers concept development, scripting, storyboarding, location scouting, filming, audio capture, editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and multi-format export. You receive finished, platform-ready files with no extra steps.
What formats will my videos be delivered in?
All videos are delivered in 4K and HD resolutions with platform-specific aspect ratios — 16:9 for YouTube and websites, 9:16 for TikTok, Reels and Shorts, and 1:1 for feed posts. You also receive raw footage archives and project files on request.
How does video content improve marketing performance?
Video content drives 5x higher engagement than static posts, lowers CPM by 25-40% due to higher platform relevance scores, and generates 60%+ more traffic in the first 30 days. Professional video builds trust faster and increases conversion rates across every channel.
